# 🛡️ MS KAVACH – Edge-Based Women Safety System

> **MS KAVACH - Mānavī Suraksha Kavach** is a camera-mounted, edge-based women safety system designed to detect distress situations, provide immediate local deterrence, and enable rapid alert escalation for faster emergency response.

---

## 📖 Overview

Women safety remains a major concern, especially in public spaces where immediate assistance may not be available. Traditional safety solutions often rely on manual intervention, such as panic buttons or mobile applications.

**MS KAVACH** addresses this challenge by automatically detecting potential distress situations, triggering a local deterrence buzzer, and notifying a control room for verification and response.

This repository presents a **Streamlit-based simulation dashboard** demonstrating the proposed workflow and system behavior.

## ⚙️ Proposed Workflow

1. Camera continuously monitors the environment.
2. Edge AI analyzes captured frames.
3. Distress situation is detected.
4. Local deterrence buzzer is activated.
5. Alert is sent to the control room.
6. Operator verifies the received image.
7. Location is identified.
8. Alert is escalated to the nearest response units.

---

## 🧠 Proposed Machine Learning Approach

For future deployment, the system is designed to incorporate lightweight edge AI models such as:

- CNN-based distress classification
- Behaviour analysis
- Gesture recognition (including protective gestures like Palm Tuck)
- Edge-based inference for low latency

> **Note:** This repository demonstrates the system workflow through simulation. Full ML model integration is part of future deployment.
